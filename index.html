How Ordering Works

We make it easy to get the desserts you love for any occasion.

1. Choose your treats

Browse our menu and add items to your order. Customize flavors, sizes, and decorations.

2. Request with 4–5 days notice

Most orders need 4–5 days. Party Packages require at least 1 week advance notice.

3. Review & secure payment

We review your request, send your final price, then a secure Stripe link for the 50% deposit to confirm your order.

4. Pickup or Delivery

Fresh on your chosen date. Delivery available in Miami area for a small additional fee.

Please list any allergies in the order form. We take dietary needs seriously.

Important Information

Policies & Payments

Payments

After we review your request, we'll send your final price and a secure Stripe payment link for the 50% non-refundable deposit to confirm your order.

The remaining balance is due the day before pickup or delivery. We'll reach out via text or Instagram within 24 hours.

Cancellations

Deposits are non-refundable. Please give 48 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule.

Allergies & Delivery

Please let me know about any food allergies before ordering. I can offer delivery for an extra fee depending on location.

Ordering Timeline

Small orders need 3–5 days notice. Party packages require 1–2 weeks. No same-day orders.

Liability

Once your order is picked up or dropped off, all responsibility for its care transfers to you. I'm not liable for any issues after that.
